About Me

With a sharp eye for aesthetics and a skill for understanding what users really need, I thrive on collaborative brainstorming and rapid prototyping to create solutions that truly resonate with both users and stakeholders. Collaborative teamwork is my thing—I love bouncing ideas around with my teammates and getting direct feedback from users to refine designs. Once I dive into Figma, it’s hard pulling me away!
